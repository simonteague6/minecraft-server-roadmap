Phase 1 — Day One

0/9 −

Survive the first nights and lay the groundwork. Speed matters less than not losing your stuff.

  • Punch trees → wood tools → stone tools
    Full stone kit within the first 10 minutes.
  • Beds for every player
    Kill sheep or shear wool. Set spawns immediately — and sleeping skips phantoms.
  • Communal starter shelter
    Near spawn, with a shared chest and a couple of furnaces.
  • 🚜 FARM #1: Basic crop farm
    Till near water; plant every seed, carrot, and potato you find. Food security first.
  • 🚜 FARM #2: Animal pens
    2+ each of cows, sheep, chickens, pigs. Leather → books later. Egg + lava = classic first auto cooked-chicken farm.
  • Full iron tools + shield + bucket + flint & steel
    Cheap stopgap while you mine: copper tools/armor (added 1.21.9) don't compete with your iron needs.
  • Carry a water bucket at all times
    MLG saves, lava-to-obsidian, and it never stops being useful.
  • Locate a village — write down the coords
    Free food, beds, and your future villager workforce. Don't burn it down.
  • Set up a shared coordinates system
    Signs at spawn, a book & quill, or a pinned Discord message. Future-you says thanks.
⚙️

Phase 2 — Iron, Villagers & Infrastructure

0/8 −

The industrial revolution. The iron farm and trading hall are the two biggest unlocks in the entire game — everything after this gets easier.

  • Mine diamonds (Y = -53 to -59)
    Diamond pickaxe minimum — you need it for obsidian.
  • 🚜 FARM #3: Iron farm
    THE early-game unlock: infinite hoppers, rails, anvils, buckets, armor. 3+ villagers with beds + a zombie scare = golem spawns. Build one, share it.
  • 🚜 FARM #4: Villager breeder + trading hall
    Cure zombie villagers for 1-emerald trades. Priorities: librarian (Mending!), farmer (emerald printer), armorer, toolsmith, cleric.
  • 🚜 FARM #5: Sugarcane farm
    Paper → books → bookshelves, plus emeralds via librarians. Observer-piston designs are the standard.
  • 🚜 FARM #6: Tree farm + bamboo farm
    Even a manual bonemeal grid counts. Bamboo = best renewable furnace fuel + scaffolding.
  • Enchanting table + 15 bookshelves
    Get Mending, Efficiency, Unbreaking, and Protection books from your librarians.
  • 🚜 FARM #7: Mob/XP farm
    Spawner-based (easy) or dark room (medium). XP, string, bones, arrows. Build far from where people AFK — mob caps are shared.
  • Raid a Trial Chamber as a group
    Trial keys, wind charges, breeze rods — and a shot at the Heavy Core for the Mace.
🔥

Phase 3 — The Nether

0/9 −

Blaze rods, gold, pearls, and the fastest travel in the game. Bring fire resistance and don't look the piglins in the eye without gold armor.

  • Build the portal — then secure BOTH sides
    Cobblestone-proof the portal room before anything else. Ghast fireballs open portals to chaos.
  • Find a Nether fortress → 🚜 FARM #8: Blaze farm
    Blaze rods power brewing and eyes of ender. A spawner-based blaze farm is safe and future-proof.
  • Brewing stand + core potions
    Fire Resistance (mandatory for nether farms), Strength, Healing.
  • Loot a bastion
    Netherite upgrade smithing template, snout armor trim, Pigstep.
  • Barter with piglins for ender pearls
    Wear at least one gold armor piece or they attack on sight.
  • 🚜 FARM #9: Gold/XP farm
    The bartering engine of the mid-game: gold → pearls, string, fire res potions. Best XP before the End. Portal-based or nether-roof designs.
  • Nether hub network (roof or ice roads)
    8:1 travel — connect every player's base through the Nether.
  • Grow Happy Ghasts 🎈
    Dried ghasts spawn in Soul Sand Valley fossils (or craft: 8 ghast tears + soul sand); rehydrate in water ~20 min. A flying platform that carries 4 players — the best SMP build tool in years.
  • Start collecting wither skeleton skulls
    Skull-hunting trips now, or 🚜 FARM #10: wither skeleton farm (hard — fortress crossroads design). You need 3 skulls soon.
✨

Phase 4 — Gear Up & Find the Stronghold

0/5 −

Don't fight the dragon in half-enchanted armor. Kit everyone out, then triangulate.

  • Full enchanted diamond armor + tools for EVERY player
    Protection IV, Mending, Unbreaking III across the board.
  • Ancient debris (Y = 15) → netherite weapon + pickaxe
    Bed-mining or TNT. Needs the upgrade template from a bastion (duplicate it: 7 diamonds + netherrack).
  • Craft eyes of ender → triangulate the stronghold
    Blaze powder + ender pearls. Throw, walk, repeat.
  • Forward base at the stronghold
    Beds, a chest of backup gear, and a secured portal room.
  • Stock the dragon kit
    Golden apples, Slow Falling + Healing potions, bow + arrows (for perching), building blocks, water bucket.
🐉

Phase 5 — The End & the Dragon

0/5 −

Make it a server event — the dragon is dramatically easier (and more fun) with everyone there.

🏆

Free the End

Crystals first (bow them down, climb the caged ones), then hit the dragon when it perches. Beds are not a strategy here — they're an explosion.

  • ⚔️ Defeat the Ender Dragon together
    The headline server event. Screenshot it.
  • Collect the dragon egg
    Piston or torch trick. Decide who keeps it BEFORE the fight.
  • End city + End ship → Elytra
    Enter an End gateway (pearl or build through). Eventually one elytra per player, plus shulker shells.
  • 🚜 FARM #11: Enderman farm
    Endermite-on-a-leash design, 128+ blocks off the main island. Fastest XP in the game — 0 to 30 levels in about a minute — plus infinite pearls.
  • 🚜 FARM #12: Shulker shell farm
    Shulkers duplicate when hit by shulker projectiles. Infinite shulker boxes change everything about server logistics.
💎

Phase 6 — Post-Dragon Power Curve

0/6 −

The dragon was the midpoint, not the end. Now you build the machines that make everything else trivial.

  • ⚔️ Kill the Wither → Nether Star → beacon
    3 skulls + 4 soul sand. Fight it underground or at bedrock; bring the whole server.
  • Full-power beacon pyramid (164 blocks)
    Your iron farm makes this trivial. Classic move: Haste II beacon at the shared quarry.
  • 🚜 FARM #13: Raid farm
    ⚠️ Reworked in 1.21: Bad Omen now comes from drinking ominous bottles, dropped only by outpost captains outside a raid. Pre-1.21 tutorials are obsolete — use a current design. Still the best emerald + totem source.
  • 🚜 FARM #14: Guardian farm
    Ocean monument, modern nether-portal designs skip the drain. XP rivaling the enderman farm + bulk prismarine and sea lanterns.
  • Activate a conduit
    8 nautilus shells + Heart of the Sea. Pairs with the guardian farm project. Nautilus mounts (1.21.11) make ocean work easier too.
  • Finish the wither skeleton farm
    Skulls for more beacons; wither roses from Wither kills feed rose-based killing chambers.
🏆

Phase 7 — Late Game & Megaprojects

0/9 −

Everything from here is optional — and it's the part people play for years.

  • 🚜 FARM #15: Automatic tree farm + crafter super smelter
    The Crafter (1.21) turns raw farms into finished-product factories: auto sticks, auto TNT, auto bread.
  • 🚜 FARM #16: Copper golem sorting hall
    New in 1.21.9: dump items into copper chests, golems sort into matching wooden chests. A storage room with zero redstone.
  • 🚜 FARM #17: Copper farm (drowned)
    Copper golems, chests, lanterns, and building demand make copper real again. Tridents + nautilus shells as a bonus.
  • 🚜 FARM #18: Honey/bee farm
    Honey blocks for redstone, honeycomb to wax all that copper. Campfire under the hive keeps the bees calm.
  • 🚜 FARM #19: Froglight farm
    Frogs eat small magma cubes → froglights. Frog variant sets the color: temperate = ochre, cold = verdant, warm = pearlescent. Build at a bastion magma cube spawner.
  • Sniffer side quest 🐢
    Brush suspicious blocks in warm ocean ruins for sniffer eggs → torchflowers & pitcher plants. Fun, not a power farm.
  • Explore the Sulfur Caves (new in 26.2)
    Sulfur cubes are passive, bucketable, and grow with slimeballs; potent sulfur geysers launch you upward. No farm meta yet — invent one.
  • 🚜 FARM #20: Creeper/gunpowder farm
    If you haven't already: elytra rockets forever. Trapdoor-ceiling design with cats, unchanged for years. (Build earlier if rockets run low!)
  • Megabuilds
    Shopping district, diamond economy, mob museum, perimeter farms. Happy Ghast highly recommended as the group scaffold.

The Completion Checklist

0/10 −

What "finishing Minecraft" actually means on a Java SMP. The trophy wall.

  • Elytra + full netherite for every player
  • Max beacon at every major base ("Beaconator")
  • Mace + Heavy Core ("Over-Overkill": 50 hearts in one hit)
    Heavy Core from Trial Chamber ominous vaults.
  • Loot an Ancient City (Swift Sneak, Silence trim)
    Beating the Warden = bragging rights. Sneaking past = wisdom.
  • All 18 armor trims ("Smithing with Style")
    The rare ones: Spire, Snout, Rib, Ward, Silence, Vex, Tide, Wayfinder.
  • Every music disc
    Now includes Creator, Precipice, Tears, Lava Chicken, and Bounce (26.2).
  • "Adventuring Time" — visit every biome
    Elytra + a stack of rockets. Sulfur Caves count now.
  • "Two by Two" — breed every breedable mob
    Includes armadillo, sniffer, and camel these days.
  • "How Did We Get Here?" — every status effect at once
    The definitive end-game party trick. Harder than ever with the 1.21 effects added.
  • 💯 100% advancements
    The true "we finished the game" flag. Re-summon the dragon (4 end crystals) so latecomers get theirs too.

💠 Multiplayer Ground Rules

  • Shared spawn town, separate bases. Communal farms and portal hub at spawn; personal bases 500+ blocks out.
  • One iron farm, one trading hall — shared. Duplicates waste effort and can hurt spawn rates.
  • Coordinate AFK sessions. Mob caps are shared per dimension — someone caving nearby tanks your farm rates.
  • Divide the tech tree. One player on villagers, one on the Nether, one on storage/redstone, one on building. Rotate.
  • Bosses are server events. Dragon and Wither with everyone online, scheduled like a raid night.
  • Decide the awkward stuff day one: who keeps the dragon egg, whether TNT-duping is allowed, and the no-grief policy.
  • Use the player locator bar and a shared Happy Ghast for group builds — both recent additions made for exactly this.
